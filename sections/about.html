About VistaKine

Our Mission

VistaKine is dedicated to making abstract mathematical and physical concepts tangible through interactive 3D visualizations.

We believe that by engaging multiple senses and allowing direct manipulation of concepts, students can build stronger mental models and deeper understanding.

How to Use This Book

Navigate through chapters using the sidebar. Each section contains explanations and interactive 3D visualizations that you can manipulate to explore concepts.

For the best experience, we recommend completing chapters in order, as concepts build upon each other.
